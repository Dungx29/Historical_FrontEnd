25 most important events in world history

This new history raises exciting questions and puts events into new perspectives to stimulate real thinking about history rather than accepting that the past as set in stone. History isn’t just names and dates, but a range of decisions and actions that often turn on the smallest circumstance. The Super Summary analyzes a few events in depth but most are put into their historical framework so the reader discern where and how all of this action escorts us to the present day. If history seems dull, pick up The Super Summary to discover that Western History is alive with controversy and consequence.Not all historical moments are created equal. Some of these moments have had a significant impact on the world we live in today. In fact, you can say that our world would not be the way it is today if it wasn’t for these historical moments.
